About

Hi! My name is Stephanie, and I’m a 24-year-old Californian currently living in Oslo, Norway :)

After graduating from UC Davis with a B.Sc. in Global Disease Biology, I realized that I’d rather build stuff on my computer than dissect the dead.

Thankfully, my biology degree has never held me back from technical or creative spaces. As a conversion rate optimization (CRO) specialist, I now use both my creative and scientific competencies to design, develop, and analyze webpages and A/B tests for clients.

When I’m not working, I enjoy doodling, practicing the guitar, and staring in admiration at my sixteen plants—many of which can be found scattered around this site. I also take daily walks along the Oslofjord to compensate for my addiction to Freia chocolate.
